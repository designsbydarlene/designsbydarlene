    Hi there! I'm Darlene,


    a

    passionate web and graphic designer

     based in New York, NY.

    Originally from Honolulu, HI,

     I made the move to pursue my dream job in design.

    My journey in web design

    began at the age of 12

    when I became a Myspace guru, thanks Tom! I have been professionally

    designing for over 8 years

    . Over the years, my passion for art, technology, and computers merged, leading me into the world of digital design.

    I hold a

    Master of Science in Computer Science,

    where I honed my skills in

    UX/UI and HCI

    . Understanding these components has greatly influenced my approach to web design, allowing me to create

    intuitive and user-friendly experiences.

    Aside from my academic achievements, I've had the privilege of supporting

    local businesses and non-profits

     through my freelance design work, helping them establish their online presence and reach their goals.

    I am 

    thrilled about the prospect of new opportunities

    to collaborate and innovate in the world of design. Every project brings its own unique challenges and rewards, and I'm eager to tackle them head-on and

    deliver exceptional results.
